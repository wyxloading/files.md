Let's be lazy

We hate productivity and planning. We hate being organized. Relax.

The only thing that matters is your calmness.

Let's save your mental energy.

It drains if you keep stuff in your head.

What stuff?

Notes, when you have thoughts and ideas

Jot down ideas to clear your mind and free up mental space.

Tasks, when there's something we have to do

Keep track of your to-dos without cluttering your mind.

Checklist items, when there's something to buy or read

A simple list for what you need to get done, bought, or read.

Emotions, when we are stressed or acting out our patterns

Capture your feelings to understand them and stay calm.
